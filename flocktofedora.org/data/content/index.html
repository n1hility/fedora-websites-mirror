Schedule

Flock 2017 is a more action-oriented event than previous years. This affects the types of session proposals that will be accepted. A team status report is less likely to be accepted than a collaborative team working session or a workshop.

We will be announcing the list of accepted proposals soon. The Flock 2017 planning committee will focus on ensuring the right folks can attend these working sessions, so expect a productive and energizing event!

Our CFP for session proposals closed on June 15, 2017. However, registration remains open! Register now - you are welcome to register to attend whether or not you have proposed a session.

Event Dates

Flock will begin the morning of Tuesday, August 29. Sessions will continue each day until midday on Friday, September 1. You should plan to arrive on Monday, August 28 and to depart no sooner than Friday afternoon for the best experience.

Venue and Hotel

The Resort and Conference Center at Hyannis is both the conference venue as well as the conference hotel.

  • A $139/night* rate per double, queen, or king room is available. You will need to use the "Group Attendee" code "FLOCK" on the hotel reservation form to book your room at the Flock rate.
  • A $169/night* double occupancy (under 18 free) discounted rate is available the weekend before and the weekend after.
  • If you are booking by phone, please state that you are attending the Flock 2017 Conference.
  • July 26 is the cut-off for our reserved block. If reserving for yourself, please book before then!

Please Note: If you receive financial assistance to attend Flock, the organizers will book your lodging for you.

* 11.7% lodging tax applies.
