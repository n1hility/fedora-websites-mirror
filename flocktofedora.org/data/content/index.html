Schedule

Flock 2017 is a more action-oriented event than previous years. This affects the types of session proposals that will be accepted. A team status report is less likely to be accepted than a collaborative team working session or a workshop.

The Flock 2017 planning committee will focus on ensuring the right folks can attend these working sessions, so expect a productive and energizing event!

Our CFP is open from May 1 - June 1, 2017.

Transportation

There are many transportation options to get to, from, and around Hyannis and Cape Cod.

The closest international airport is Logan International Airport in Boston, MA.

The Plymouth & Brockton bus line has a direct bus from Logan to the Hyannis Transportation Center; it is a 2-hour trip one-way.
