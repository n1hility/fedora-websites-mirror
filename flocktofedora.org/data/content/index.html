Schedule

The Flock 2017 schedule is now available!

Flock 2017 is a more action-oriented event than previous years. This affects the types of session proposals that were accepted. The scheduled slots favor collaborative team working sessions and workshops over team status reports.

Event Dates

Flock will begin the morning of Tuesday, August 29. Sessions will continue each day until midday on Friday, September 1. You should plan to arrive on Monday, August 28 and to depart no sooner than Friday afternoon for the best experience.

Registration Information

Registration for Flock is still open! Register now - you are welcome to register to attend whether or not you have proposed a session.

Attendees who register and pay the registration fee by July 24, 2017 will be included in the count for items that have to be confirmed in advance. This includes lunch and the t-shirt. If you register and/or pay after this date you may not receive all of the benefits of registration.

Requests for financial assistance are handled via the registration system. Applications for Financial Assistance filed after July 14, 2017 are unlikely to be funded as there are only limited funds available.

Venue and Hotel

The Resort and Conference Center at Hyannis is both the conference venue as well as the conference hotel.

  • A $139/night* rate per double, queen, or king room is available. You will need to use the "Group Attendee" code "FLOCK" on the hotel reservation form to book your room at the Flock rate.
  • A $169/night* double occupancy (under 18 free) discounted rate is available the weekend before and the weekend after.
  • If you are booking by phone, please state that you are attending the Flock 2017 Conference.
  • The Cape Cod Resort and Conference Center will charge your credit card immediately for one night as a deposit which will be applied to the last night of your stay. The remainder of the charges will be paid at checkout.
  • July 26August 4 - Extended is the cut-off for our reserved block. If reserving for yourself, please book before then!

Please Note: If you receive financial assistance to attend Flock, the organizers will book your travel arrangements (flight, hotel, bus) for you.

* 11.7% lodging tax applies.
